Investment Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

1. Introduction

This document outlines the terms and conditions governing the investment in Electric Vehicle (EV) charging ports ("Ports") offered by ChargePace ("the Company"). By submitting an application, you ("the Investor") agree to be bound by these terms.

2. The Investment

The Investor agrees to provide capital for the purchase, siting, and installation of an EV charging Port as described in the selected investment package. The Company will be responsible for all aspects of operation, management, and maintenance of the Port.

3. Earnings and Payouts

Earnings are generated from the usage fees of the charging Port. The Investor is entitled to a share of the net profits, calculated after deducting operational costs, transaction fees, and maintenance reserves. Payouts will be made on a monthly basis to the Investor's designated account.

4. Risks

All investments carry risk. The returns are not guaranteed and are subject to market conditions, including but not limited to:

  • Location performance and utilization rates.
  • Fluctuations in electricity costs.
  • Regulatory changes affecting the EV industry.
  • Technological obsolescence.

The Investor acknowledges that they may not recoup their full initial investment.

5. Application and Payment Process

Submission of an application does not guarantee acceptance. The Company reserves the right to approve or reject any application at its sole discretion. Upon approval, the Investor will receive a formal contract and payment instructions. The investment is considered active only after the full payment has been received and confirmed by the Company.

6. Termination

Details regarding the termination of the agreement, lock-in periods, and potential buy-back options will be outlined in the formal investment contract provided to the Investor upon application approval.

7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all financial and operational details confidential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can i trust ChargePace?

Yes, since ChargePace was created as a subsidiary of Tesla, investors have set up over 30,000 ports and earned more 200 milion USD in profits.

What exactly am I investing in?

You are purchasing ownership of a specific, individual EV charging port at one of our high-traffic locations. While we manage the operation and maintenance, you are the owner of that physical asset and receive the direct revenue it generates.

Is this a completely passive investment?

Yes, 100%. Our team handles everything from site selection and installation to ongoing maintenance, software updates, and customer payment processing. Your role is simply to invest and track your earnings.

How do I get paid?

All revenue generated by your charging port, minus our transparent management and electricity fee, is automatically calculated and deposited into your account. You can withdraw your earnings at any time through your investor dashboard.

How do you choose the locations for the chargers?

Our locations are selected based on a rigorous data-driven process. We analyze traffic patterns, local EV adoption rates, proximity to major venues (like airports and corporate parks), and long-term area growth to ensure high utilization and consistent returns.

Can I sell my charging port in the future?

Yes. We are developing a secondary marketplace that will allow you to list your charging port for sale to other investors on the ChargePace platform, providing you with liquidity for your investment.
